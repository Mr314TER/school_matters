Bruce Lee

(Chinese: 李小龍; born Lee Jun-fan, 李振藩; November 27, 1940 until July 20, 1973) was a Hong Kong and American martial artist, actor and philosopher. He was the founder of Jeet Kune Do, a hybrid martial arts philosophy drawing from different combat disciplines that is often credited with paving the way for modern mixed martial arts (MMA).
